UEFA Nations League

Participation and strong performances in the UEFA Nations League have made the team proud.

Central Asian Championship

Kazakhstan FC has been competitive in regional tournaments, often leading the way.

Asian Cup Qualification

Before joining UEFA, Kazakhstan competed in the AFC.

  • Qualified: 2004
  • Best Performance: Quarterfinals (2004)
